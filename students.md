  <div class="section-head"><h2>Academic genealogy</h2></div>
  <div class="prose">
    <p>My advisor at the University of Virginia was Kevin Sullivan, whose advisor was David Notkin. My academic genealogy goes all the way back to <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Edsger_W._Dijkstra">Edsger Dijkstra</a>, who won the Turing Award in 1972.</p>
    <p>In a prior life, I worked at Bell Labs India, the R&amp;D organization of Lucent Technologies, where I was interested in bandwidth and power optimization in mobile ad hoc and sensor networks as they scale up. I still keep up with these topics.</p>
