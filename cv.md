      <h2>Summary</h2>
      <p>Computer scientist and academic leader working across programming languages, software engineering, and trustworthy AI. Known for the Ptolemy programming language and for Boa, an infrastructure for data-driven software engineering at ultra-large scale. Current work focuses on the modularity and trustworthiness of AI-enabled systems.</p>

      <h2>Education</h2>
      <ul>
        <li>Ph.D., Computer Science, University of Virginia</li>
        <li>M.S., Computer Science, University of Virginia</li>
        <li>B.Tech., Computer Science and Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology (BHU) Varanasi (formerly IT-BHU)</li>
      </ul>

      <h2>Academic and leadership appointments</h2>
      <ul>

        <li>Kingland Professor and Chair, Department of Computer Science, Iowa State University (2019–2024)</li>
        <li>Founding Professor-in-Charge, Data Science Programs, Iowa State University (2017–2019)</li>

      </ul>
