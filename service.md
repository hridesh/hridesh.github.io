<section class="section">
  <div class="prose">
    <p>I have served the international and national communities through several roles. One of the most satisfying was leading the design and execution of the first ACM hybrid conference, <a href="https://2021.splashcon.org/">SPLASH 2021</a>, organized both as a physical event in Chicago and virtually, with more than 800 participants. It combined virtual and in-person Q&amp;A sessions and introduced features such as hybrid coffee breaks, a mirrored schedule for geographic inclusion, and time bands in the program. As a steering committee member for the NSF Midwest Big Data Hub, I advised on sustainability, governance, and program guidance.</p>
  </div>
</section>
